How we think about apps

Free means free

Not freemium. Not free-with-ads. Not free-for-now. These apps will never ask you for money. The business model is simple: there isn't one.

Real apps, not websites

Save any app to your home screen and it works like a native app. Full offline support, smooth animations, responsive on any screen size.

Mobile-first, everywhere

Every app works on your phone, tablet, and desktop. Use it from a mobile browser, save it to your home screen, or open it on your laptop.

Private by default

No accounts, no tracking, no analytics. Your data stays on your device. Nothing leaves your browser.

Open source

Every app is open source. Read the code, verify our claims, contribute improvements, or fork it and make your own.

Open platform

ProAppStore

  • Paid apps with advanced features
  • May mirror free apps with extras
  • Cloud sync, collaboration, AI
  • Priority support
  • Sustainable development

The two platforms are intertwined. You can always switch between the free and pro version of any app. If you're happy with free — stay there. If you want more, pro is one click away. No pressure, no dark patterns.
