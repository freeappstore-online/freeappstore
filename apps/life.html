Features

Country-based Estimates

Uses country-specific life expectancy data for more accurate projections.

How It Works

Life is a connected app that uses Firebase for authentication and data storage. Sign in to save your goals and settings across devices. The life expectancy calculator uses WHO data adjusted by your personal health inputs.

Install it as a PWA (save to home screen) for a native app experience with offline support.

Privacy

Life uses Firebase Authentication for account management. Your goals and settings are stored in your Firebase account. No data is shared with third parties, no ads, no tracking beyond what's needed for the app to function.

Open Source

Life is fully open source under the MIT license. You can inspect the code, report bugs, or contribute improvements.
