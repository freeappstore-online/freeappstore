How It Works

Meetup is a connected app with a shared backend. Create a free account to browse events and RSVP. The free version gives you full discovery and attendee features.

The app uses the same backend as the Pro version — your account and data carry over seamlessly if you upgrade.

Pro Version

Want more? The Pro version adds event creation, group management, messaging, and analytics. Same account, same data — just more tools.

Privacy

Meetup requires an account to function. Your profile and RSVP data is stored on the shared backend. No data is sold or shared with third parties, no ads, no tracking beyond what's needed for the app to function.

Open Source

Meetup is fully open source under the MIT license. You can inspect the code, report bugs, or contribute improvements.
