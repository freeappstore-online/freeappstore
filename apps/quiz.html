How It Works

Quiz runs entirely in your browser. Swipe right for true/correct and left for false/incorrect. Your progress and scores are saved to localStorage so they persist between sessions.

Install it as a PWA (save to home screen) for a native app experience with offline support.

Privacy

Quiz collects zero data. No analytics, no tracking, no cookies. All your progress stays on your device in localStorage. Nothing is ever sent to a server.
