How It Works

Puzzle runs entirely in your browser. Puzzles are generated dynamically with progressive difficulty. All scores and progress are saved to localStorage so they persist between sessions.

Install it as a PWA (save to home screen) for a native app experience with offline support.

Privacy

Puzzle collects zero data. No analytics, no tracking, no cookies. All your scores stay on your device in localStorage. Nothing is ever sent to a server.

Puzzle is fully open source under the MIT license. You can inspect the code, report bugs, or contribute improvements.
